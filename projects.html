Projects

Sustainable Packaging for Cardiac Devices

Designed recyclable thermoformed packaging using a DIY vacuum system. Created custom 3D-printed molds in SolidWorks and executed a DOE to ensure material strength and sterilization compliance, saving over $400K in projected costs.

REMpillow – EEG-Based Sleep Tracker

Collaborated with electrical engineers to build a biosensor-integrated pillow that tracks REM cycles via EEG. Focused on sensor integration, signal processing, and usability improvements beyond commercial sleep trackers.

Electric Car Project

Designed and built an Arduino-based electric car using L298N motor driver boards. Programmed using logical structures (if/else, boolean operators) to perform obstacle navigation tasks in a controlled test environment.

Geartrain Design Project

Engineered a 3-layer accessory gearbox with 18 spur gears and 12 shafts to meet aerospace specifications. Performed in-depth stress and fatigue analysis on 4130 Q&T steel components to achieve infinite fatigue life under dynamic loading.

Torsion Bar Suspension System Design

Created a high-strength steel torsion bar suspension system optimized for performance and sustainability. Used SolidWorks and mechanical analysis to verify safety factors, material choice, and durability under torsional loads.
